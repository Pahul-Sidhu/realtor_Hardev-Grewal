Uninterrupted panoramic western views of the city, Downtown Vancouver, Burrard Inlet and North Shore Mountains! Perfectly found in the hills, this 3200 sq.ft home has 4 bedrooms and 4 baths with a formal living room, dining room & family room that opens up to a huge sundeck. The lower level of the home is your own personal oasis with a private outdoor lap pool and mature landscaping. Quiet, serene getaway just minutes to Downtown Vancouver. Great location in Burnaby North close to transit, trails, parks, Brentwood Shopping Center, and fine cafes & restaurants on Hastings St.
